# Prompt do banner (ChatGPT / gerador de imagem)

Banner 16:9 unindo a marca Água Camelo (mascote camelo "Camelo") com o bolão da Copa 2026.
Cole no ChatGPT. Para fidelidade do mascote, anexe a imagem em `~/camel-mascot/`.

---

```
Create a high-resolution horizontal banner illustration (16:9 aspect ratio) in
a "premium sports poster / flat vector with sticker finish" style. It should be
energetic and bold, uniting the water brand AGUA CAMELO with the excitement of a
2026 World Cup office betting pool ("bolao").

MAIN CHARACTER:
A charismatic, confident camel mascot (named "Camelo") in a heroic soccer-star
pose, placed on the RIGHT THIRD of the banner. He wears a stylized soccer jersey
and rests one foot on a ball. The ball is not ordinary: it is a fusion of a
soccer ball and a large crystal-clear water droplet, symbolizing water. His
expression is vibrant, friendly and triumphant, full of personality.

SCENE AND COMPOSITION:
Wide cinematic banner layout. Stylized night stadium in the background with a
packed crowd and bright lights, subtly blended with a desert oasis on the horizon
(soft dunes and a small palm tree) to connect the camel/desert world with the
World Cup atmosphere. The ground is made of water splashes and waves shaped like
a football pitch. Confetti and sparks of light rising. A subtle golden trophy in
the background. Keep the LEFT SIDE more open and clean to leave room for text.
Strong sense of motion: energy lines and a dynamic water splash around the ball.

STYLE AND TECHNIQUE:
Flat vector illustration with solid color blocks, clean outlines, sharp straight
corners, screenprint/sticker finish and a light poster texture. Bold, modern and
contemporary. NO heavy gradients, NO blur, NO photorealism. Striking, graphic
look.

COLOR PALETTE (follow strictly, Agua Camelo brand identity):
- Dominant background: black (#000000)
- Deep blue (#023DA5) and vivid blue (#0372FF) for water and elements
- Electric cyan (#0EFBE3) as the primary highlight
- Lime green (#ABFF5A) and touches of green (#00906C) for energy and the pitch
- Pink (#FF6FFF) as a punchy accent
- White (#FEFEFE) for highlights
Use the colors in solid, high-contrast blocks for maximum visual impact.

TYPOGRAPHY / TEXT (minimal and clearly legible, on the open left side):
Include, in a bold condensed sans-serif, the text "AGUA CAMELO" at the top and
"BOLAO DA COPA 2026" below it, integrated into the poster like a badge. Keep the
text short and correctly spelled.

MOOD: World Cup adrenaline, celebration, brand pride and fun among coworkers.
Final result: a "wow", banner-worthy artwork, good enough for a website header
and a team t-shirt.

AVOID: real brand logos (FIFA, Adidas, Nike, etc.), specific country flags,
realistic human faces, watermarks, messy or unreadable text, generic AI
aesthetics, blurry gradients.
```

## Dicas
- Mascote fiel: anexe a imagem do Camelo (`~/camel-mascot/`) + "Use the attached character as the reference for the mascot, keeping the same style."
- Texto torto: peça "redo keeping everything the same, only fix the text to AGUA CAMELO and BOLAO DA COPA 2026", ou gere sem texto e adicione depois.
- Acento: "AGUA"/"BOLAO" sem acento de propósito (gerador erra menos). Com acento: "ÁGUA"/"BOLÃO".
- Outros formatos: header bem panorâmico = 21:9; story/celular = 9:16; post = 4:5.
